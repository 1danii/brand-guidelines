---
title: Logo Guidelines
description: Our logo represents the brand identity and should be used consistently across all materials. Follow these guidelines to maintain brand integrity.
---

<Columns>
  ## Clear Space
  
  Always maintain adequate clear space around the logo. The minimum clear space is equal to the height of the "A" in the logo.
  
  ## Minimum Size
  
  The logo should never be reproduced smaller than 24px in height for digital applications or 10mm for print.
