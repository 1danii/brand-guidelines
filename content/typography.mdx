  # Typography
  
  Our typographic system uses Avenga Haffer for body text and Avenga Reckless for display and headings. This combination creates a distinctive and readable brand voice.
  
  ## Avenga Haffer
  
  Used for all body copy, UI elements, and general text. Weights available: Regular (560) and Bold (730).
  
  ## Avenga Reckless
  
  Used for headlines and display text. Weights available: Headline (480) and Regular (450).
  
  ## Type Scale
  
  - **Heading 1**: 48px / Avenga Reckless Headline
  - **Heading 2**: 32px / Avenga Reckless Regular
  - **Body**: 16px / Avenga Haffer Regular
  - **Small**: 14px / Avenga Haffer Regular
